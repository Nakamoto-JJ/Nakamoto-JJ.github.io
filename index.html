Short Bio

I am currently working toward the Ph.D. degree with the School of Computer Science, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China.

I received the National Scholarship, National Encouragement Scholarship, Sichuan Provincial Comprehensive Quality Level-A Certificate, and Chongqing Municipal Merit Student.
